Parcae

A Fictional Podcast

Branding Design; Editorial Design; Multimedia

BA Communication Design - 2020

Parcae is a fiction written and recorded by us, where we started by asking ourselves: "What would life be if we knew the day of our death since birth?". In this fiction, we follow 7 different characters with 7 different perspectives about this topic, living their last day on this spaceship called "Parcae".
